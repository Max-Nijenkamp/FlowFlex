# ESG & Sustainability — Opportunity Research

Web-researched gaps in the ESG/carbon-accounting space (Watershed, Persefoni, Workiva, Greenly).
Candidate differentiators for if/when FlowFlex builds this domain. All speculative — **UNVERIFIED**.

## Candidate differentiators

1. **SMB-affordable pricing** — Watershed at ~$100K/yr and Workiva at $75K–$250K+/yr are absurd for a $5M
   ARR company; a suite-bundled ESG module priced for SMEs is the core wedge. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
2. **No-expertise usability** — Watershed/Persefoni cater to experienced sustainability pros; a guided,
   template-driven flow for teams with no ESG staff is an accessibility gap. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
3. **Emissions + disclosure in one** — Workiva does disclosure but not emissions analysis; competitors do
   emissions but narrower disclosure; a combined build avoids the two-tool stitch. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
4. **European regulatory depth** — Watershed is US-focused with thin coverage of EUDR / PPWR / CSRD; an
   EU-first build with those frameworks native is a differentiator. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
5. **Auto-pull activity data** — Greenly's EcoPilot auto-pulls data while Persefoni demands granular manual
   collection; FlowFlex owning Finance + Procurement data can auto-populate footprint. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
6. **Scope 3 via built-in procurement** — Scope 3 is ~80% of B2B SaaS footprint and hardest to measure;
   FlowFlex's native supplier/spend data gives a structural Scope 3 advantage. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
7. **Assurance-ready from year 1** — SEC + CSRD increasingly require limited/reasonable assurance;
   baking data lineage + auditor access in from the start is a selling point. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
8. **Broader S + G coverage** — Persefoni has narrow social/governance metrics and needs pairing with other
   tools; covering E + S + G in one module removes that gap. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
9. **Hybrid spend + activity accounting** — Greenly wins mid-market with hybrid activity+spend accounting;
   supporting both methods lowers the data-quality barrier for early-stage measurers. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
