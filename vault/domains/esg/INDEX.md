# ESG & Sustainability

FlowFlex's ESG panel provides a purpose-built sustainability management suite — carbon footprint tracking, ESG KPIs, initiative management, regulatory reporting (GRI, CSRD, TCFD), stakeholder reports, and supply chain assessments — replacing tools like Watershed and Plan A.

**Filament Color:** Green
