# Business Travel

FlowFlex's Travel panel manages the full corporate travel lifecycle — from employee request submission through manager approval, booking records, policy enforcement, and post-trip expense reporting — replacing dedicated tools like TravelPerk and Concur.

**Panel:** `travel` — `/travel`
**Filament Color:** Cyan
