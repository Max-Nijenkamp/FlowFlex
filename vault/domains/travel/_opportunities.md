# Business Travel — Opportunities

Web-researched (2025-2026) gaps in corporate travel & expense tooling (SAP Concur, Navan, TravelPerk). Candidate differentiators for a FlowFlex SMB travel module. Speculative items marked **UNVERIFIED**.

## Sourced candidates

| # | Opportunity | Evidence | Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Truly unified travel + expense on one platform.** TravelPerk switchers "want truly unified travel and expense management with a single platform." Fits FlowFlex's all-in-one thesis directly (Finance owns expenses in-house). | Navan Perk-alternatives | 2026 |
| 2 | **Modern UX vs Concur's dated interface.** Concur "criticized for a dated UX" and "highly intricate architecture" that hurts smaller orgs. A clean SMB-first UX is the wedge. | Navan Concur review; Perk | 2025-26 |
| 3 | **Real-time spend control + policy at point of purchase.** Concur lacks "real-time spend control"; buyers want "integrated corporate cards that enforce policy at the point of purchase." | Navan Concur review; Perk-alternatives | 2026 |
| 4 | **Transparent, predictable pricing.** Concur's "opaque pricing" and per-report/per-transaction fees "lead to unpredictable total costs." Flat SMB pricing differentiates. | Navan Concur review | 2025 |
| 5 | **Card-agnostic booking / fare transparency.** Navan "locks users into their card system" and leaves travellers "blind on fare classes." A card-agnostic, fare-transparent flow is an opening. | Rippling Navan-alternatives | 2026 |
| 6 | **Easy trip changes.** Navan users report "limited options and difficulties changing trip details cause unnecessary hassle." Frictionless rebooking/changes is a differentiator. | Rippling; Otto the Agent | 2026 |
| 7 | **SMB-sized without enterprise complexity.** Both Navan and Concur "leave gaps"; Concur's complexity is "a significant hurdle for smaller organizations." The under-served 50-500 seat band is the target. | Navan; Perk | 2025-26 |
| 8 | **Duty-of-care for smaller firms.** Traveller safety/location tooling is typically enterprise-gated. **UNVERIFIED** as a stated SMB complaint but plausible unmet need. | Inferred | 2026 |
