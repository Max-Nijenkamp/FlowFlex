# Risk Management

FlowFlex's Risk panel manages the full enterprise risk lifecycle — risk identification, assessment, control mapping, compliance monitoring, and board reporting — replacing Archer and LogicManager for SMB and mid-market governance teams.

**Panel:** `risk` — `/risk`
**Filament Color:** Red
