# Risk Management — Opportunities

Web-researched (2025-2026) gaps in enterprise risk / GRC tooling (LogicGate, AuditBoard, Vanta, Drata). Candidate differentiators for a FlowFlex risk module aimed at the mid-market. Speculative items marked **UNVERIFIED**.

## Sourced candidates

| # | Opportunity | Evidence | Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Mid-market fit between "compliance automation" and "enterprise GRC."** Vanta targets 1-2 frameworks; LogicGate/AuditBoard target 3-5 with cross-mapping. Mid-market buyers "navigating multiple frameworks" find "the biggest names not a good fit." A right-sized multi-framework tool is the gap. | vCSO.ai; Vanta resources | 2026 |
| 2 | **Low configuration time to value.** "LogicGate requires significant configuration time to get value" with less out-of-the-box automation than Vanta/Drata. Fast, opinionated setup differentiates. | vCSO.ai | 2026 |
| 3 | **Affordable pricing.** AuditBoard runs "$50,000-$200,000+/year"; Vanta is "expensive for all features." A transparent SMB/mid-market price point is underserved. | PlanetCompliance; SmartSuite | 2026 |
| 4 | **EU data residency.** AuditBoard has "no EU data residency"; explicitly flagged for EU mid-market B2B. FlowFlex could offer EU hosting as a wedge. | vCSO.ai | 2026 |
| 5 | **Extensibility to internal audit + policy for smaller orgs.** AuditBoard's scope "may not be extensible to internal auditing and policy management for smaller organizations." A broader-but-lighter suite fits. | vCSO.ai / Vanta comparison | 2026 |
| 6 | **Gentler onboarding / learning curve.** Vanta switchers cite "a steep learning curve beginning with onboarding" and "problematic integrations." UX + reliable integrations are differentiators. | SmartSuite Vanta alternatives | 2026 |
| 7 | **Native audit-evidence from an all-in-one suite.** FlowFlex already owns the activity log, HR, and support data GRC tools scrape via integrations — evidence collection could be first-party. **UNVERIFIED** as a stated buyer need. | Inferred from integration-pain theme | 2026 |
| 8 | **Customisation depth for complex environments.** "In very large, complex environments Vanta's model may not fully match customization needs." **UNVERIFIED** relevance to 50-500 seat target. | Vanta resources | 2026 |
