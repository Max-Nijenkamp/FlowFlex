# CRM & Sales

CRM & Sales is the revenue-generating domain of FlowFlex. It covers the full customer relationship lifecycle — from initial contact through deal pipeline, quoting, contracting, and post-sale account management. The CRM panel lives at `/crm`.

## Navigation Groups

- **Pipeline** — Deals, Pipeline, Forecasting, Quotes, Contracts
- **Contacts** — Contacts, Customer Segments, Territory Management
- **Activities** — Activities, Email Integration, Sales Sequences, Deal Rooms
- **Intelligence** — Revenue Intelligence, Loyalty
- **Settings** — Stages, Products, Playbooks

## Modules

| Module | File | Module Key | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Contacts | [[contacts]] | `crm.contacts` | Contact and company records, relationship mapping, communication history |
| Deals | [[deals]] | `crm.deals` | Deal records: value, stage, close date, products, probability |
| Pipeline | [[pipeline]] | `crm.pipeline` | Visual pipeline — custom page with drag-and-drop deal cards |
| Quotes | [[quotes]] | `crm.quotes` | Quote from deal, line items, discounts, PDF, e-signature |
| Contracts | [[contracts]] | `crm.contracts` | Customer contracts, renewal dates, value, e-signature |
| Activities | [[activities]] | `crm.activities` | Calls, emails, meetings logged against contacts/deals |
| Email Integration | [[email-integration]] | `crm.email` | Bi-directional email sync, emails auto-linked to contacts |
| Customer Segments | [[customer-segments]] | `crm.segments` | Dynamic segments based on attributes — for campaigns and playbooks |
| Territory Management | [[territory-management]] | `crm.territory` | Sales territory assignment, rep ownership, territory reporting |
| Forecasting | [[forecasting]] | `crm.forecasting` | Sales forecasts, quota tracking, weighted pipeline |
| Sales Sequences | [[sales-sequences]] | `crm.sequences` | Automated outreach sequences, email cadences, call reminders |
| Deal Rooms | [[deal-rooms]] | `crm.deal-rooms` | Shared digital space for buyer and seller — documents, Q&A, action plans |
| Loyalty | [[loyalty]] | `crm.loyalty` | Customer loyalty program, points, tiers, rewards catalog |
| Revenue Intelligence | [[revenue-intelligence]] | `crm.revenue-intelligence` | AI deal health scoring, at-risk deals, win/loss analysis |
| Appointment Scheduling | [[appointment-scheduling]] | `crm.scheduling` | Public booking pages, round-robin team scheduling, calendar sync (Google + Outlook), Stripe paid bookings |
| Referral Program | [[referral-program]] | `crm.referrals` | Unique referral links per customer, conversion detection, configurable rewards, referral leaderboard |
