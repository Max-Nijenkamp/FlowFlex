# Partner & Channel — Opportunity Research

Web-researched gaps in the partner/channel (PRM) space (PartnerStack, Impartner, Crossbeam, Kiflo).
Candidate differentiators for if/when FlowFlex builds this domain. All speculative — **UNVERIFIED**.

## Candidate differentiators

1. **No revenue-scaling tax** — PartnerStack's pricing scales with revenue + adds transaction fees, so it
   gets very expensive as programmes succeed; a flat suite-bundled price avoids punishing growth. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
2. **Native CRM (not Workato)** — PartnerStack's CRM integrations depend on Workato; FlowFlex owning the CRM
   makes deal ↔ registered-deal sync native and real-time. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
3. **Modern UI + fast implementation** — Impartner is "expensive and heavy" with month-long implementation
   and a dated backend UI; a modern, self-serve build is a clear wedge for SMBs. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
4. **Payout + attribution together** — Crossbeam can't generate referral links, track clicks, or pay
   partners; PartnerStack pays but is rigid; combining attribution + payout in one avoids the two-tool
   split. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
5. **Flexible portal / co-sell** — PartnerStack's portal is rigid and not built for complex co-sell or
   multi-tier ecosystems; a configurable portal supporting tiers/co-sell is a differentiator. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
6. **Advanced commission customization at SMB tier** — SMB-focused Kiflo has basic reporting and limited
   commission customization as you scale; offering richer commission rules without enterprise pricing fills
   the gap. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
7. **SMB affordability + full features** — the core market challenge is balancing affordability with
   necessary features without excessive complexity; suite bundling is structurally positioned to do both. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
8. **Built-in enablement/LMS** — partner onboarding/certification usually needs a separate LMS; FlowFlex's
   own LMS makes enablement native. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
9. **Native payout via Finance** — commission accrual → payout run through the suite's Finance domain avoids
   the external payout infrastructure Crossbeam lacks entirely. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
