# Customer Success

FlowFlex's Customer Success panel provides the tools a CS team needs to proactively manage customer health — health scoring, success playbooks, shared success plans, churn risk detection, and onboarding milestone tracking — replacing Gainsight and ChurnZero.

**Panel:** `cs` — `/cs`
**Filament Color:** Blue
