# Professional Services (PSA)

FlowFlex's PSA panel gives consulting and professional services firms a purpose-built delivery management suite — project phases, resource allocation, profitability tracking, billable time, client reporting, and capacity planning — replacing fragmented tools like Harvest and Teamwork.

**Panel:** `psa` — `/psa`
**Filament Color:** Fuchsia
