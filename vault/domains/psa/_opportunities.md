# Professional Services Automation — Opportunities

Web-researched (2024-2026) gaps in PSA tooling (Kantata/Mavenlink, Scoro, Mavenlink). Candidate differentiators for a FlowFlex PSA module. **Note the Projects-domain overlap** — several of these are only worth building if PSA reuses Projects rather than re-implementing it. Speculative items marked **UNVERIFIED**.

## Sourced candidates

| # | Opportunity | Evidence | Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Real-time dashboards, not export-driven reporting.** Firms cite Kantata reporting "relying on exports vs real-time dashboards" and "difficulty around dashboards, reporting, insights." Live utilisation/margin dashboards are the top ask. | G2 Kantata reviews; Scoro blog | 2025 |
| 2 | **Fluid week-to-week resource scheduling.** Kantata's resource scheduling "feels rigid for day-to-day changes." Drag-and-drop reallocation that survives daily churn is a differentiator. | Operating.app; Scoro | 2025 |
| 3 | **Non-billable time visibility.** "Non-billable time visibility being patchy, skewing utilization and forecasts." Capturing and surfacing non-billable work cleanly improves forecast accuracy. | Operating.app Kantata alternatives | 2025 |
| 4 | **Estimating that beats spreadsheets.** Firms report "estimating feeling faster in spreadsheets than in-product tools." An estimating UX people actually prefer over Excel is a real gap. | Operating.app | 2025 |
| 5 | **Native accounting integration (no manual steps).** Xero/QuickBooks integrations "require manual work"; Scoro's QuickBooks/Outlook links "aren't smooth." FlowFlex owning Finance in-house sidesteps this entirely. | Scoro blog; Operating.app | 2025 |
| 6 | **Gantt / dependency views.** Scoro "does not support Gantt charts"; firms with delivery-plan needs miss them. Reuse Projects' scheduling to cover this cheaply. | Scoro blog | 2025 |
| 7 | **Deep resource-forecasting for smaller firms.** Scoro's planning is "lighter than Kantata's"; Kantata is heavy/expensive. A mid-weight option sized for 50-500 seat firms is underserved. | Scoro blog; Operating.app | 2025 |
| 8 | **Resource visibility is the #1 PSA purchase driver in 2026.** Validates leading with utilisation/capacity rather than billing. Supports scoping order if built. | Operating.app / Viewpoint 2026 | 2026 |
| 9 | **Unified T&M + fixed-fee + WIP in one billing flow.** Implied across sources; standalone tools split these. **UNVERIFIED** as a distinct buyer complaint. | Inferred | 2025 |
