# Real Estate & Property — Opportunities

Web-researched (2025-2026) gaps in property-management software (AppFolio, Buildium, Yardi). Candidate differentiators for a FlowFlex property module. Speculative items marked **UNVERIFIED**.

## Sourced candidates

| # | Opportunity | Evidence | Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Maintenance built from the ground up, not bolted on.** Legacy PMS grew by bolting maintenance onto accounting platforms — mobile work orders are "clunky and slow," SLA tracking "doesn't exist," preventive schedules are manual. A native, mobile-first work-order engine is the clearest gap. | Oxmaint migration guide | 2025 |
| 2 | **Real-time SLA tracking + predictive maintenance.** Explicitly listed as features "modern property managers expect" but that legacy tools lack. | Oxmaint | 2025 |
| 3 | **One unified platform vs siloed logins.** Industry "runs on a fragile mesh of spreadsheets, manual re-entry, and siloed systems" needing separate logins for accounting, maintenance, tenant comms. Unification is a top review request — FlowFlex's all-in-one thesis fits directly. | Oxmaint; TenantCloud | 2025 |
| 4 | **Accessible pricing for small landlords.** AppFolio's 50-unit minimum and ~$298/mo floor "eliminate it for smaller landlords"; add-on costs balloon. A transparent small-portfolio tier is underserved. | AppFolio/Yardi comparisons; TenantCloud | 2026 |
| 5 | **Predictable cost as portfolio grows.** Buildium switchers cite "cost predictability as unit count and transaction volume grow." Flat/seat pricing rather than per-unit + per-transaction differentiates. | Buildium alternatives guides | 2026 |
| 6 | **Modern UI.** Yardi Breeze "less modern than Buildium or AppFolio"; newer entrants win on UX. FlowFlex's Switchboard+ skin is an advantage here. | Yardi comparisons | 2026 |
| 7 | **Flexible accounting for complex portfolios.** Buildium "accounting reporting does not fit more complex portfolio structures." | Buildium alternatives | 2026 |
| 8 | **Responsive support during incidents.** Buildium switchers cite "support responsiveness during incidents"; Yardi phone support gated behind higher tiers. **UNVERIFIED** as a product (vs ops) differentiator. | Buildium/Yardi comparisons | 2026 |
