# Real Estate & Property

FlowFlex's Real Estate panel provides property portfolio management for companies that own or lease commercial and residential properties — property register, lease management, tenant records, maintenance, rental billing, and IFRS 16 lease accounting — replacing Yardi and Re-Leased.

**Panel:** `realestate` — `/realestate`
**Filament Color:** Stone
