# Events Management

FlowFlex's Events panel manages the full lifecycle of corporate events — creation, attendee registration, speakers, sponsors, on-the-day check-in, and post-event analytics — replacing Eventbrite and Cvent for in-house event teams.

**Panel:** `events` — `/events`
**Filament Color:** Rose
