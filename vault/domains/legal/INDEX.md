# Legal & Compliance

One panel for contracts, legal matter management, compliance obligations, risk tracking, e-signatures, document review, IP, and regulatory monitoring — replacing Ironclad, Clio, and ContractPodAi.

**Panel:** `legal` — `/legal`
**Filament color:** Amber

---

## Modules

| Module | Key | Description |
|---|---|---|
| [[contracts]] | legal.contracts | Contract repository, metadata (parties, value, term, renewal), and status workflow |
| [[matter-management]] | legal.matters | Legal matter tracking, outside counsel management, cost tracking, and documents |
| [[compliance-calendar]] | legal.compliance | Compliance obligation calendar, deadline tracking, responsible owner, and evidence upload |
| [[risk-register]] | legal.risk | Legal risk register with risk scoring and mitigation notes |
| [[e-signatures]] | legal.e-signatures | E-signature request workflow: send doc, signatories sign, signed copy stored |
| [[document-review]] | legal.document-review | Legal document review workflow: draft, review, approve, execute |
| [[ip-tracking]] | legal.ip | Intellectual property register: patents, trademarks, copyrights, and renewal dates |
| [[regulatory-tracking]] | legal.regulatory | Regulatory change tracking, impact assessment, and implementation tasks |
