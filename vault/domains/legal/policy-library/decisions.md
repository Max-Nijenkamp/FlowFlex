# Policy Library — Decisions

- **Acknowledgements are per version.** Publishing a changed body bumps the version and resets acks — re-acknowledgement of material changes is provable *(assumed)*.
- **Audience is department-scoped or all.** `audience` jsonb of department ids; null = whole company.
- **Reminders target only unacknowledged.** Weekly cadence; naturally stops when acknowledged *(assumed)*.
