# Community & Social

FlowFlex's Community panel gives companies a branded community space — discussion forums, events, member profiles, badges, and membership tiers — without the cost of a standalone platform like Circle.so or Discourse.

**Panel:** `community` — `/community`
**Filament Color:** Purple
