# Finance & Accounting

Finance & Accounting is the financial management domain of FlowFlex. It covers the complete accounting stack — from the general ledger and journal entries through invoicing, expense management, AP/AR, bank reconciliation, budgets, and financial reporting. The finance panel lives at `/finance`.

## Navigation Groups

- **Ledger** — General Ledger, Journal Entries, Fixed Assets
- **Invoicing** — Invoices, Accounts Receivable
- **Expenses** — Expenses, Accounts Payable
- **Budgets** — Budgets, Cash Flow
- **Reporting** — Financial Reporting, Tax Management, Multi-Currency

## Modules

| Module | File | Module Key | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| General Ledger | [[general-ledger]] | `finance.ledger` | Chart of accounts, journal entries, trial balance |
| Invoicing | [[invoicing]] | `finance.invoicing` | Customer invoices: create, send, track payment, recurring |
| Expenses | [[expenses]] | `finance.expenses` | Expense submissions, approvals, categories, receipts |
| Budgets | [[budgets]] | `finance.budgets` | Department and project budgets, variance tracking |
