# HR & People

HR & People is the people operations domain of FlowFlex. It covers the complete employee lifecycle from recruitment and onboarding through daily time and leave management, performance reviews, compensation, and eventually succession planning. The HR panel lives at `/hr` and is separated from all other domain panels.

## Navigation Groups

- **Employees** — Profiles, Self-Service, Org Chart, Onboarding, Recruitment
- **Leave** — Leave Management, Time & Attendance, Shift Scheduling
- **Payroll** — Payroll, Global Payroll, Compensation & Benefits, Employee Benefits
- **Analytics** — HR Analytics, DEI Metrics, Workforce Planning, Workforce Forecasting
- **Settings** — Leave Types, Departments, Pay Grades
