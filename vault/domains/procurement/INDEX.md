# Procurement & Spend Management

FlowFlex's Procurement panel automates the purchase-to-pay cycle — requisition approval, PO creation, preferred supplier catalog, goods receipt, and spend analytics — replacing Coupa and Procurify for mid-market organisations.

**Panel:** `procurement` — `/procurement`
**Filament Color:** Amber

| Module | Key | Nav Group | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[purchase-requisitions]] | procurement.requisitions | Requests | Internal purchase requests: item, justification, budget check, approval |
| [[purchase-orders]] | procurement.orders | Orders | PO creation from approved requisitions, supplier confirmation, GRN matching |
