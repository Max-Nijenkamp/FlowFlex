# Whistleblowing & Ethics — Opportunity Research

Web-researched gaps in the whistleblowing/ethics-hotline space (NavEx, Whispli, EQS, FaceUp, Whistlelink).
Candidate differentiators for if/when FlowFlex builds this domain. All speculative — **UNVERIFIED**.

## Candidate differentiators

1. **SMB-affordable, transparent pricing** — NavEx custom-quotes well above €5,000/yr with no public
   pricing or trial; a suite-bundled channel at Whistlelink-tier (€49–€199/mo) undercuts the incumbent. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
2. **Fast self-serve deployment** — NavEx has long implementation cycles; the directive deadline pressure
   rewards a same-day, no-sales-call setup. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
3. **EU-directive-native, not a GRC bolt-on** — for NavEx the EU rules are one slice of a global GRC suite;
   a build with the six directive requirements native out of the box is cleaner for EU SMEs. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
4. **Zero-knowledge / strong encryption** — Whispli notably *lacks* zero-knowledge encryption; a genuine
   zero-knowledge evidence vault is a trust differentiator. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
5. **Directive timers built in** — auto-enforced 7-day acknowledgement + 3-month feedback deadlines with
   reminders remove the manual compliance-tracking most tools leave to the handler. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
6. **Phone hotline option** — Whispli lacks a phone hotline; adding a voice/callback intake covers a channel
   incumbents charge extra for. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
7. **Multilingual intake** — table stakes for EU (multiple official languages); FaceUp/Whistlelink lead
   here, so parity is required, not optional. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
8. **Suite-native but walled** — being inside FlowFlex gives instant SSO for handlers + HR org routing,
   while a hard confidentiality wall keeps reporter identity out of every other domain. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
9. **Included, not per-seat** — AllVoices/Whispli tier by seats/company size; bundling the channel with the
   suite removes seat math for a compliance obligation every 50+ company simply must have. (2025, UNVERIFIED)
