# Workplace & Facility

FlowFlex's Workplace panel manages the physical workspace — office space registry, desk and room bookings, visitor management, maintenance requests, and occupancy analytics — replacing point solutions like Robin and Envoy.

**Panel:** `workplace` — `/workplace`
**Filament Color:** Lime
