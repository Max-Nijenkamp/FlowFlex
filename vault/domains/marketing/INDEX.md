# Marketing

One panel to plan, execute, and measure every marketing activity — campaigns, email, content, social, SEO, landing pages, and lead capture — without stitching together HubSpot, Mailchimp, Buffer, and Ahrefs.

**Panel:** `marketing` — `/marketing`
**Filament color:** Pink

| Module | Key | Description |
|---|---|---|
| [[campaigns]] | marketing.campaigns | Multi-channel campaign planning, execution, and tracking |
| [[email-marketing]] | marketing.email | Email campaigns: templates, list management, send, open/click tracking |
| [[landing-pages]] | marketing.landing-pages | Landing page builder with forms and lead capture integration |
| [[lead-capture]] | marketing.leads | Lead forms, scoring, and routing to CRM contacts |
