# Field Service Management

FlowFlex's Field Service panel manages the full lifecycle of field operations — work order creation, technician dispatch, parts inventory, customer asset tracking, SLA enforcement, and job invoicing — replacing ServiceTitan and FieldAware for in-house field service teams.

**Panel:** `field` — `/field`
**Filament Color:** Orange
