# Field Service — Opportunity Research

Web-researched gaps in the field-service-management space (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, IFS,
ServiceFusion). Candidate differentiators for if/when FlowFlex builds this domain. All speculative — **UNVERIFIED**.

## Candidate differentiators

1. **One system, not disconnected tools** — 45% of FSM professionals waste 11+ hrs/week chasing info across
   disconnected systems; a suite where dispatch, invoicing, and inventory already share a DB removes that
   reconciliation gap. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
2. **FSM that fits real dispatch/payroll flow** — tools "look right in the demo and bleed productivity in
   production" when they don't match how dispatch/credentialing/payroll actually run; native HR + payroll
   integration is the wedge. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
3. **Deliverable invoicing/email** — a cited complaint: FSM-sent invoice emails land in spam and go unpaid;
   sending invoices through FlowFlex's own transactional email/domain reputation fixes cash-flow leakage. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
4. **Predictable SMB pricing** — SMBs with 1–15 techs pay $60–$5,250/mo across fragmented tools; a bundled
   suite price avoids the per-tech + per-module stack. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
5. **Native CRM + field in one** — Jobber/Housecall are strong standalone but re-sync customers with an
   external CRM; FlowFlex owning CRM makes customer/job data single-source. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
6. **Balanced AI onboarding** — a 2026 Software Advice survey found 72% of buyers take a balanced approach
   to adopting AI features; assistive (scheduling, triage) rather than forced AI matches buyer appetite. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
7. **Shared parts inventory** — reusing the suite's Inventory domain for van/warehouse stock avoids a
   separate parts system and double-entry on consumption. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
8. **Mobile-first tech surface** — Housecall Pro's edge is mobile-first for owner-operators; a PWA tech app
   sharing suite auth matches that without a separate login. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
9. **SLA/service-agreement tracking bundled** — recurring service agreements + SLA entitlement are often
   higher-tier add-ons; including them lowers the entry bar for maintenance-contract businesses. (2026, UNVERIFIED)
