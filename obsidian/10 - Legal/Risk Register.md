# Risk Register

Identify, score, assign, and mitigate business risks. Board-ready risk reporting.

**Who uses it:** Legal, compliance, senior management
**Filament Panel:** `legal`
**Phase:** 5

## Events Fired

- `RiskFlagRaised` → consumed by Legal, [[Notifications & Alerts]] (notifies risk owner, creates mitigation task)

## Features

- **Risk identification and categorisation** — strategic, operational, compliance, financial, reputational
- **Likelihood × impact matrix scoring** — standard risk scoring methodology
- **Risk ownership assignment** — named accountable owner per risk
- **Mitigation action tracking** — tasks linked to risk record
- **Board-level risk report export** — formatted PDF for board packs
