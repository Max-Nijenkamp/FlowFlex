# Insurance & Licence Tracking

Register for all business insurance policies and regulatory licences. Never let one lapse.

**Who uses it:** Legal, finance, operations
**Filament Panel:** `legal`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Business insurance policy register** — type, insurer, policy number, coverage, expiry
- **Regulatory licence register** — trade licences, professional certifications, FCA, SRA, etc.
- **Expiry alerts** — configurable notice period
- **Document vault** per policy
- **Renewal reminders** to responsible owner
