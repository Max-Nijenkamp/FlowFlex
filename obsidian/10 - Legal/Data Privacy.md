# Data Privacy (GDPR / CCPA)

Handle data subject requests, manage consent, and maintain your Article 30 register.

**Who uses it:** Legal, compliance, DPO
**Filament Panel:** `legal`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Data subject request (DSR) handling workflow** — DSAR, right to access, right to erasure
- **Right to erasure automation** — deletes PII across all modules when triggered
- **Consent management** — record and track consent per purpose
- **Data processing register (Article 30)** — GDPR register of all processing activities
- **Cookie consent banner** — embeddable on workspace's website
- **Privacy policy generator** — template-based policy generation
