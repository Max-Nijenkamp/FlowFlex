# Policy Management

Publish company policies, track employee acknowledgement, and version-control everything.

**Who uses it:** Legal, HR, compliance, all employees
**Filament Panel:** `legal`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Company policy publishing** — with version control
- **Employee acknowledgement** — must-read confirmation required
- **Compliance tracking** — who has signed, who hasn't, per policy
- **Scheduled review reminders** per policy
- **Integration with [[HR Compliance]]** — policy sign-off tracked against employee records
