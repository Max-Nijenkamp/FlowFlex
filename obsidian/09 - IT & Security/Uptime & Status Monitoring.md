# Uptime & Status Monitoring

Monitor services, publish a public status page, and manage incidents.

**Who uses it:** IT team
**Filament Panel:** `it`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Monitor internal or client-facing services**
- **Public status page** — hosted on FlowFlex subdomain (e.g. `status.yourcompany.com`)
- **Incident management workflow** — structured incident declaration and resolution
- **Alert rules** — email, Slack, SMS on downtime
- **Postmortem templates** — structured incident review
