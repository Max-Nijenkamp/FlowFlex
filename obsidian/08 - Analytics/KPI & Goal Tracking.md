# KPI & Goal Tracking

Define company KPIs and cascade them down. Traffic-light status keeps everyone aligned.

**Who uses it:** Leadership, managers, all employees
**Filament Panel:** `analytics`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Define company KPIs** and cascade to departments and individuals
- **Traffic-light status** — green/amber/red per KPI
- **Trend charts** over time
- **Threshold alerts** — notify when KPI drops below target
- **Benchmark comparisons** — vs last period, vs target
