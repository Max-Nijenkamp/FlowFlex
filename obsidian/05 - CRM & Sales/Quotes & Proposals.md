# Quotes & Proposals

Build quotes from the product catalogue. Client views, signs, and it converts to an invoice automatically.

**Who uses it:** Sales team, account managers
**Filament Panel:** `crm`
**Depends on:** [[Contact & Company Management]], [[Product Catalogue]] (if active)
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Quote builder** — from product catalogue, custom line items, rates, discounts
- **Discount rules** — percentage or fixed amount per line or total
- **Convert quote to invoice in one click** — links to [[Invoicing]]
- **View tracking** — know when client opens the quote (email open tracking)
- **E-signature on acceptance** — client signs the quote digitally
- **Auto-creates deal** in [[Sales Pipeline]] when quote is accepted
