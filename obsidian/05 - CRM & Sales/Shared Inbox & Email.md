# Shared Inbox & Email

Shared team inbox for sales@ and support@ addresses. All emails are logged to the CRM automatically.

**Who uses it:** Sales team, customer support
**Filament Panel:** `crm`
**Depends on:** [[Contact & Company Management]]
**Phase:** 3

## Features

- **Shared team inbox** — sales@, support@, info@ — any shared address
- **Email sequences** — drip campaigns for sales outreach
- **Templates** — saved reply templates
- **Open and click tracking** — see when the recipient opened the email
- **Auto-log all emails** to contact timeline in [[Contact & Company Management]]
- **Inbox assignment rules** — auto-assign incoming emails based on subject/sender
