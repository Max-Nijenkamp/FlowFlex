# Contact & Company Management

360° contact and company records. Every interaction your team has with a contact is logged and visible in one timeline.

**Who uses it:** Sales, support, account management, marketing
**Filament Panel:** `crm`
**Depends on:** Core
**Phase:** 3
**Build complexity:** Medium — 2 resources, 5 tables

## Features

- **360° contact record** — full profile: name, company, title, email, phone, address, social profiles
- **360° company record** — full profile with all associated contacts, deals, tickets
- **Full activity timeline** — emails, calls, meetings, notes, deals, tickets, invoices — all in one view
- **Custom fields** — workspace-defined fields for any contact or company attribute
- **Tags and segmentation** — free-form tags and list membership
- **Data enrichment** — LinkedIn / Clearbit integration (auto-populate profile data)
- **Duplicate detection and merge** — find and merge duplicate records
