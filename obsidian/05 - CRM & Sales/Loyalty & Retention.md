# Loyalty & Retention

Points, referrals, and churn prediction. Keep your best customers and win back the ones drifting.

**Who uses it:** Marketing, sales, customer success
**Filament Panel:** `crm`
**Depends on:** [[Contact & Company Management]]
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Points / rewards programme** — award points for purchases, engagement, referrals
- **Referral tracking** — track customer-referred leads
- **Churn prediction score** — ML-based risk flag per customer
- **Win-back campaign triggers** — fires to [[Email Marketing]] module on churn risk
- **Tier management** — Bronze / Silver / Gold tiers with different benefits
