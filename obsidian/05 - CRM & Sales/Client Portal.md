# Client Portal

Branded self-service portal your clients log into. No FlowFlex branding — fully white-labelled.

**Who uses it:** Clients (external), account managers
**Filament Panel:** `crm`
**Depends on:** [[Contact & Company Management]], ideally [[Invoicing]], [[Document Management]] active
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Project status view** — clients see live status of their projects
- **Invoice payment** — clients pay invoices online directly
- **Document sharing** — shared files visible to clients with appropriate permissions
- **Support ticket raising** — client submits and tracks support tickets
- **Approval request signing** — client signs documents via [[Document Approvals & E-Sign]]
- **No FlowFlex branding** — fully white-labelled with workspace's own logo and colours
