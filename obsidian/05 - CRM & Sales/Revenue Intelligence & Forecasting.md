# Revenue Intelligence & Forecasting

AI-powered revenue forecasting and pipeline intelligence. Know what you're going to close this quarter — before the quarter ends.

**Who uses it:** Sales managers, RevOps, CFO
**Filament Panel:** `crm`
**Depends on:** [[Sales Pipeline]], [[AI Sales Coach]], [[Finance Overview]]
**Phase:** 6

---

## Features

### Forecast Models

- **Rep-submitted forecast** — reps manually commit deals to the forecast
- **AI weighted forecast** — pipeline × probability (AI-adjusted, not just stage %)
- **History-based forecast** — regression model on past quarter performance
- **AI best case / worst case** — range based on deal health and activity signals

All four shown side-by-side on the forecast dashboard.

### Forecast Accuracy Tracking

- Compare forecast submitted at start of quarter vs actual close
- Accuracy % per rep, per manager, per team
- Trend: is forecast accuracy improving?
- Highlight over-optimistic reps (consistently submit too high)
- Use historical accuracy to automatically discount rep submissions

### Pipeline Analytics

- Pipeline coverage ratio: pipeline value ÷ quota target (healthy = 3–4×)
- Stage conversion rates (week-over-week trend)
- Pipeline created vs pipeline closed per period
- Average sales cycle by stage, by deal size, by rep
- Pipeline by source: inbound, outbound, partner, referral
- Pipeline at risk: % of pipeline with no activity in 7+ days

### Quota Management

- Set quotas per rep per period (annual, quarterly)
- Quota attainment live dashboard: % of quota closed
- Pace tracker: on track to hit quota at current close rate?
- Team quota rollup: see whole team performance against target
- YTD attainment chart

### Revenue Metrics

- Booked revenue (confirmed closed won)
- Weighted pipeline value
- Average deal size trend
- Win rate trend
- Sales cycle trend
- Revenue per source channel
