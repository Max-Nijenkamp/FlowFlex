# Quality Control & Inspections

Digital inspection checklists, pass/fail scoring, and non-conformance management.

**Who uses it:** Quality managers, inspectors, operations
**Filament Panel:** `operations`
**Depends on:** Core
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Digital inspection checklists** — customisable per inspection type
- **Pass/fail scoring** — weighted criteria
- **Photo evidence capture** — mobile camera during inspection
- **Non-conformance report (NCR) raising** — from any failed item
- **Corrective action tracking** — assign and track resolution
- **ISO audit readiness package** — pre-built templates for ISO inspections
