# Health, Safety & Environment (HSE)

Incident reporting, risk assessments, and safety compliance — all on mobile.

**Who uses it:** All staff (for incident reporting), safety managers, HR
**Filament Panel:** `operations`
**Depends on:** Core
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Incident reporting** — mobile and desktop, immediate after an event
- **Risk assessments** — digital risk assessment forms
- **Hazmat / COSHH register** — hazardous substance register
- **Toolbox talk logging** — attendance tracking for safety briefings
- **Safety induction completion tracking** — new employee safety training sign-off
- **RIDDOR report generation** (UK) — auto-formatted for reporting to HSE
- **Near-miss reporting** — low-barrier form to encourage near-miss culture
