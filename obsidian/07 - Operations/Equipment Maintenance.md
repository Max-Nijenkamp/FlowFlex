# Equipment Maintenance (CMMS)

Preventive and reactive maintenance management for all physical assets.

**Who uses it:** Maintenance team, operations managers
**Filament Panel:** `operations`
**Depends on:** [[Asset Management]]
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Preventive maintenance schedules** — time-based or usage-based triggers
- **Reactive work orders** — raised from fault reports
- **Fault reporting** — mobile-friendly form for field staff
- **Technician dispatch** — linked to [[Field Service Management]] if active
- **Parts usage tracking** — deducts from [[Inventory Management]]
- **Full maintenance history** per asset
- **Downtime tracking** — log unplanned downtime per asset
