# Supply Chain Visibility

Track orders from supplier to warehouse to customer. Know where things are before customers ask.

**Who uses it:** Operations managers, procurement
**Filament Panel:** `operations`
**Depends on:** [[Purchasing & Procurement]], [[Inventory Management]]
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Order tracking** — from supplier to warehouse to customer
- **Supplier scorecards** — quality, on-time delivery, lead time performance
- **Lead time tracking** — actual vs expected delivery times
- **Delay alerts** — notify when shipment is late
- **Landed cost calculation** — total cost including shipping, duties, tariffs
