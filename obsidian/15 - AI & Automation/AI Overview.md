# AI & Automation Overview

The intelligence layer of FlowFlex. Every module gets smarter. Every repetitive task disappears. No Zapier subscription needed — automation is built in.

**Filament Panel:** `ai`
**Domain Colour:** Cyan `#06B6D4` / Light: `#CFFAFE`
**Domain Icon:** `sparkles` (Heroicons)
**Phase:** 6

## Why This Domain Exists

By 2026 every serious SaaS platform has AI baked in. FlowFlex's AI & Automation domain is the engine that ties everything together:

- **Replace Zapier/Make** — workflow automation without a third-party subscription
- **Replace Microsoft Copilot** — AI assistant deeply integrated with your actual business data
- **Replace consulting hours** — smart defaults, auto-configuration, and predictive suggestions mean customers set up themselves

## Competitive Position

| Competitor | What They Do | FlowFlex Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| Zapier | Connects external apps | FlowFlex automation works on internal data with zero latency, no per-task billing |
| Make (Integromat) | Visual workflow builder | Same depth, no external subscription, native data access |
| Microsoft Copilot | AI across Microsoft 365 | Works on YOUR business data, not just documents |
| HubSpot AI | CRM-specific AI | Cross-domain: HR + Finance + CRM + Ops in one AI context |
| Notion AI | Document writing | FlowFlex AI acts on structured business data, not just text |
