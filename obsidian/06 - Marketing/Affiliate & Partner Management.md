# Affiliate & Partner Management

Run a branded affiliate programme with automated tracking, commissions, and payouts.

**Who uses it:** Marketing team, partnership managers
**Filament Panel:** `marketing`
**Depends on:** Core, [[Finance Overview]] (for payouts)
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Affiliate portal** — branded external login for affiliates
- **Referral link generation** — unique tracking link per affiliate
- **Commission rules** — percentage, flat rate, or tiered by volume
- **Payout management** — via [[Finance Overview]] module
- **Performance reports** — clicks, conversions, revenue per affiliate
