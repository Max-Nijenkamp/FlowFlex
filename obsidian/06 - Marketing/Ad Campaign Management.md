# Ad Campaign Management

Connect your ad accounts and see spend, ROAS, and attribution in one place.

**Who uses it:** Marketing team, paid media managers
**Filament Panel:** `marketing`
**Depends on:** Core
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Google Ads account connection**
- **Meta Ads connection** (Facebook + Instagram)
- **Spend tracking** — daily/monthly budget burn
- **ROAS dashboard** — return on ad spend per campaign
- **Conversion attribution**
- **Unified spend vs organic dashboard** — compare paid and organic performance
