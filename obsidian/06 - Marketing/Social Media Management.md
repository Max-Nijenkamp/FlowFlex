# Social Media Management

Plan, approve, and publish content across all social channels from one calendar.

**Who uses it:** Marketing team, social media managers
**Filament Panel:** `marketing`
**Depends on:** Core
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Content calendar** — month/week view of all scheduled posts
- **Multi-channel publishing** — LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, X, TikTok
- **Approval workflow for posts** — content must be approved before publishing
- **Best-time suggestions** — based on historical engagement data
- **Performance analytics per channel** — reach, engagement, clicks
