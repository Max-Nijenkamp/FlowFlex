# Product Catalogue

Centralised product database. One update pushes to storefront, POS, and quoting simultaneously.

**Who uses it:** Ecommerce team, operations, sales
**Filament Panel:** `ecommerce`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Centralised product database**
- **Variants and attributes** — size, colour, SKU per variant
- **Pricing rules and tiers** — B2B vs B2C pricing, volume discounts
- **Tax codes** per product
- **Image gallery** — multiple images per product
- **Bulk import/export** — CSV/XLSX
- **Pushes to:** storefront, [[Point of Sale]], [[Quotes & Proposals]] — all from one update
