# Digital Products & Downloads

Deliver digital files automatically after purchase. No manual fulfilment.

**Who uses it:** Ecommerce team, customers
**Filament Panel:** `ecommerce`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **File delivery automation** post-purchase
- **Download limits** — set max number of downloads per purchase
- **Licence key generation** — auto-generate and deliver unique licence codes
- **Access expiry** — time-limited access to digital content
- **Bundle pricing** — group digital products into bundles
