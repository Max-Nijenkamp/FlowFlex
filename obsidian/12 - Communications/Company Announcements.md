# Company Announcements

Internal broadcast tool. Send to specific audiences and track who has read it.

**Who uses it:** Leadership, HR, managers
**Filament Panel:** `communications`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Internal broadcast** — send to all staff, specific team, or location
- **Read receipt tracking** — see who has read the announcement
- **Acknowledgement required flag** — employees must confirm they've read it
- **Announcement archive** — searchable history
