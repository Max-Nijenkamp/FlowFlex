# Meeting & Video Integration

Schedule and join video meetings without leaving FlowFlex. Meeting notes auto-created.

**Who uses it:** All employees
**Filament Panel:** `communications`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Schedule and join** Google Meet / Zoom from within the platform
- **Auto-create meeting notes** — structured note template on meeting start
- **Action item capture** — track actions from the meeting
- **Recording link stored** against project or task
