# Booking & Appointment Scheduling

Calendly-style booking pages. Clients and colleagues book time without the back-and-forth.

**Who uses it:** All employees, customers (external)
**Filament Panel:** `communications`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Booking page builder** — shareable URL for scheduling
- **Availability rules** per person or team
- **Calendar sync** — Google Calendar, Outlook (two-way)
- **Buffer times** — gaps between meetings
- **Group bookings** — multiple attendees
- **Confirmation and reminder emails/SMS**
- **Round-robin team booking** — distribute bookings across available team members
