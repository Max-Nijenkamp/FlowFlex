# External Training Requests

Employees request to attend conferences, courses, and certifications. Managers approve. L&D tracks the budget.

**Who uses it:** All employees (request), managers (approve), L&D team (track)
**Filament Panel:** `lms`
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **Employee training request form** — conference, course, certification, details, cost
- **Manager approval workflow**
- **L&D budget tracking** — total approved spend vs L&D budget
- **Post-training notes** — employee writes up learnings
- **Knowledge sharing** — post-training notes pushed to [[Knowledge Base & Wiki]]
