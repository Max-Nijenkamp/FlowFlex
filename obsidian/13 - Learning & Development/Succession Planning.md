# Succession Planning

Know who your successors are before you need them. Identify key-person dependencies.

**Who uses it:** HR, senior leadership
**Filament Panel:** `lms`
**Depends on:** [[Employee Profiles]], [[Performance & Reviews]]
**Phase:** 5

## Features

- **9-box talent grid** — performance vs potential matrix
- **Succession readiness scoring** per role — ready now / ready in 1 year / ready in 3 years
- **Key role risk flags** — single-person dependency alerts
- **Development path builder** — structured path to succession readiness
